🌟 Phase 1: What We Can Execute Now

✅ Goal:

Implement practical, locally funded and locally owned upgrades to the existing taxi and courier system while preparing for future digital infrastructure.

Key Initiatives:

  • 💳 Cashless Payment via Speedpoints
  • 📍 GPS Tracking in Taxis
  • 📹 CCTV Camera Installation

💳 Cashless Payment via Speedpoints

Objective:

Enable card payments in taxis to reduce crime and improve financial tracking.

Method:

Install mobile speedpoints (Capitec, Yoco, FNB, Nedbank)

Benefits:

💰 Less cash in circulation = safer rides
📊 Digital transaction history for drivers/owners
🏦 Financial inclusion for passengers

Implementation:

• Phase 1: 10 taxis as pilot
• Phase 2: Full rollout with local bank partnerships

📍 GPS Tracking in Taxis

Objective:

Real-time vehicle tracking to improve accountability and route efficiency.

Benefits:

🛡️ Passenger and driver safety
📈 Monitoring & analytics for BTA
📱 Foundation for app-based dispatching in future phases

Implementation:

• Affordable GPS units (R400-R900 per vehicle)
• BTA dashboard for real-time oversight

📹 CCTV Camera Installation

Objective:

Increase safety and professionalism through onboard surveillance.

Benefits:

🚨 Crime prevention & evidence collection
⚖️ Resolves disputes or claims
🤝 Builds public trust

Implementation:

• Install 360° mini cameras or dashcams
• Store data on SD cards or cloud when available

🚶‍♂️ Community-Driven Investment Model

Stakeholder Participation:

  • Taxi Owners
  • Local Businesses
  • Spaza Shops
  • Youth Entrepreneurs

Opportunities:

💰 Sponsor GPS units, speedpoints, or CCTV
🏷️ Receive branding on taxis ("Sponsored by X")
📊 Monthly reports & public recognition

Outcome:

Keeps ownership local and profits within the community.

🚚 Courier & Warehousing Expansion

"From people to parcels — Bushbuckridge moves it all."

🚚 Community-Owned Courier Fleet

Objective: Create a local courier system to handle last-mile deliveries for Takealot, Temu, SHEIN, and others.

Plan:

  • Acquire small vans, bakkies, or trucks
  • Offer stake/share to local investors (R20K+)
  • Drivers hired locally and trained

📦 Courier Service Benefits

Benefits:

⚡ Faster deliveries in rural zones
👥 Job creation & business ownership
🏫 Service schools, clinics, and small shops

Revenue Streams:

📦 Takealot/Temu last-mile delivery (R25-R55)
🏪 Local business delivery (R30-R80)
⚡ Urgent delivery premiums (R40+)

🏢 Local Warehousing Hubs

Objective:

Position Bushbuckridge as a regional fulfillment zone.

Action Points:

  • Secure warehouse spaces in Dwarsloop, Acornhoek, Cottondale, Thulamahashe
  • Train local youth as packers, sorters, dispatch runners
  • Offer logistics support to spaza shops & small e-commerce sellers

Partner Integration:

The Courier Guy, PAXI, Aramex, Takealot Pickup Points
